DJ E-LO Giving Back

A big part of why DJ E-LO began DJing was to help raise money for charity. DJ E-LO is a big supporter of the St. Jude Children's Research Hospital. Part of what makes St. Jude so great is that care is absolutely free, so no child gets refused treatment for financial reasons.
